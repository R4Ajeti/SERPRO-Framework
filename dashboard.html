SERP Bot Info:
Your SERP bots all work as a team and share the load automatically, paid bots can perform 300 keyword checks per day and your free bot 25. Both by default will attempt to check each keyword every 24 hours (this can be changed to between every 2 hours and 365 days).
If you set them too much work for one day they will start again, as soon as they can, from where they left off working through your keywords.
They will try their best not to repeatedly check any one twice before they have all been updated.
Please Note:
Projects with a higher update frequency are given priority, as we assume these are more important to you - This means that a project with a check frequency of every 24 hours will be checked in preference of any with a frequency of 3 days etc.
One side effect of this is that in extreme situations where you dont have enough search capacity, lower frequency projects may not get checked at all - if every day your entire quota is used checking higher frequency projects - you can fix this by setting all of your projects to the same check frequency.
